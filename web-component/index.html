Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Enrolling is simple. Visit our homepage, create an account or log in if you already have one, and browse the list of available courses. Once you find the course you're interested in, simply click "Enroll," and you'll be ready to start.

There is no strict time limit to complete the free courses. You can progress at your own pace and access the content at any time. However, we recommend following the suggested study plan to make the most of the learning experience.

You only need an internet connection and a device (such as a computer, tablet, or phone) to access the courses. Ensure you have an up-to-date browser and access to an email account, as we use email for communication and account recovery.

We provide technical support and academic assistance. For technical issues, you can contact our technical support team through the "Help" section in your profile. If you have questions about course content, you can participate in discussion forums or contact the instructor through the platform.
